Po-Yun Teng and Yun-Ching Tsai

Instruction

This tool is developed to advise peanut researchers or farmers on chemical application for stem rot disease management in field based on weather forecast.

Disease progress will be estimated by the instant field disease situations.

As a researcher, you could input greenhouse data for further forecast of stem rot disease of peanut.

As a farmer, you could input genotype of peanut and the observation of lesion length to get chemical suggestion.
